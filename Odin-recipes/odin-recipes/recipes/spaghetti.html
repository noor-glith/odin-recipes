Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish featuring long, thin pasta served with a savory tomato-based sauce, often accompanied by ground meat, garlic, and aromatic herbs. It’s a comforting meal enjoyed worldwide, whether served simply with marinara sauce or enhanced with rich meat sauce. Spaghetti is perfect for a quick family dinner or a cozy meal.

Ingredients

Spaghetti Description:

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish featuring long, thin pasta served with a savory tomato-based sauce, often accompanied by ground meat, garlic, and aromatic herbs. It’s a comforting meal enjoyed worldwide, whether served simply with marinara sauce or enhanced with rich meat sauce. Spaghetti is perfect for a quick family dinner or a cozy meal.

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce until well-coated.
  7. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
