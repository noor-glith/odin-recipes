Tacos

Taco Description:

Tacos are a versatile and flavorful Mexican dish made with soft or crispy tortillas filled with seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, and delicious toppings. Whether you prefer beef, chicken, or vegetarian fillings, tacos offer a satisfying meal thatâ€™s easy to customize to your taste. Enjoy them with your favorite salsa, guacamole, and a squeeze of lime for a burst of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (soft or crispy)
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Lime wedges

Instruction

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and taco seasoning, cooking until fragrant.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by layering the meat, vegetables, and toppings in the tortillas.
  5. Top with your favorite salsa, guacamole, and a squeeze of lime.
